Manufacturing for Make in India

To fulfill the "Make in India" concept initiated by the Govt. of India, we are going to manufacture certain implants and also equipments, so as to bring down the cost of treatment for the patients and make quality healthcare affordable for everyone.









We are a leading company in South India dealing with imported implants, instruments and capital equipments from various countries like Germany, Taiwan, UK, USA, Japan, mainly into Orthopedic and Neurosurgeries. The company got established on 9th June 2006 with its headquarters in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

Mission of the company is “Committed to provide quality services, meeting customer needs round the clock and make them to lead their quality life”.
